# Website Enhancement Plan: Eisen Paints (TBC)

Based on competitive analysis of industry leaders like **Hermosa Paints** and **Duracoat**, this plan outlines the roadmap for elevating the Eisen Paints digital experience.

## ✅ Completed Features
- **[📐 Paint Calculator](/calculator)**: Interactive tool for volume estimation with smart deductions.
- **[🎨 Color Visualizer](/visualizer)**: Real-time room preview using CSS blend modes.
- **[🔍 Advanced Product Filtering](/products)**: Refined discovery by Category, Surface, and Finish.
- **[📚 Resource & Support Center](/resources)**: Dedicated download hub for Catalogs, TDS, and SDS.
- **[📍 Dealer Locator](/find-a-dealer)**: Regional regional store finder with map integration.
- **[🍔 Premium Navigation](/src/components/Navbar.tsx)**: Dropdown-based "Tools" menu for a cleaner UI.

## 🚀 Future Enhancements (TBC)

### 1. Interactive AI Paint Finder
- **Concept**: A step-by-step wizard ("What are you painting?") that provides specific product recommendations.
- **Value**: Simplifies decision-making for non-professional users.

### 2. User Accounts & Favorites
- **Concept**: Allow users to save their favorite colors and calculator estimates to a personal dashboard.
- **Value**: Increases repeat visits and user engagement.

### 3. Integrated Project Planner
- **Concept**: A tool where users can upload floor plans or room photos and get a full quote including primer, topcoat, and labor estimation.
- **Value**: Bridges the gap between online browsing and physical project execution.

### 4. Live Chat / WhatsApp Integration
- **Concept**: Quick access to technical support and sales inquiries.
- **Value**: Immediate lead capture and customer support.

### 5. Multi-Language Support
- **Concept**: Adding Swahili translations for better accessibility in local markets.
- **Value**: Enhances inclusivity and brand reach.
